The Story

Built From Layer Zero

Layer Zero isn't just a name — it's a philosophy. In networking, Layer 0 is the physical layer: the actual wire, the actual signal, the real substrate everything digital depends on.

Layer Zero Labs was built on that same premise. Before software, before systems, before all the abstraction — something physical has to exist. We make that thing.

Founded in Georgia by a systems builder who got tired of slow vendors and expensive prototyping services, LZL is a precision 3D print studio focused on fast turnaround, zero-compromise quality, and genuine technical partnership with every client.

  • Anycubic Kobra 3 V2 Ace Pro — multi-color FDM, modified
  • Creality Hi Combo — high-speed FDM, multi-material, rooted
  • Ankermake M5C — precision FDM, modified
  • Longer LK5 Pro — large-format FDM, modified, Klipper
  • Fusion 360 + OnShape + Orca Slicer — full CAD and slicer pipeline
  • Full post-processing — sand, prime, paint, embed, assemble
  • Georgia-based — local pickup + nationwide shipping
